JEEVAN - "small steps toward better life"

Jeevan works against alcohol addiction. It provides monitoring and recovery process on a single plateform. It provides fun way for alcohol de-addiction. It provides simple,unique and fun games for the user (including Augmented Reality based games). There is no age limits. It's aim is to target large audience and spreading awareness of the harmful effects to be a sober. It has a database which having the data of real victims verified by the nearest alcohol de-addiction center or govt. hospitals .

Two-STEP Signup Process

Two-step signup where both victim and saathi have to register within the app. Saathi can be anyone like any family member or any close friend etc. , who knows about user's daily routine.

Games

After signing up user just have to play various games (including Augmented Reality based games) and do some gamified activities which are specially designed to make them feel relax and calm. It’ll contain relaxing background sound effects as well. Indirectly, these games will be diverging victim’s mind from alcohol and releasing their stress and frustration. Saathi will also ensure that the games are played by the victim himself/herself.

Reward Points

To ensure continuous engagement of victims, they will receive rewards for completing game levels and scoring points. They can use these reward points to pay electricity bills , water bills , etc. Monthly lucky draw include various government-aided offers like free consultation to doctors, recharge coupons, discount on electricity bills, exciting gifts etc.

Role Of Saathi

Saathi will help in monitoring the process. The main role of the saathi will be to answer some simple questions about victim’s behaviour changes, daily alcohol consumption etc. on weekly basis in the app itself. This weekly information will be used in monitoring process.
